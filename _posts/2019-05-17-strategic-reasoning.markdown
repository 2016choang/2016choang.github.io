I have been conducting research with the [Strategic Reasoning Group](https://strategicreasoning.org/) for over a year now, exploring topics in multi-agent systems, agent-based simulation, computational finance and reinforcement learning. I am very fortunate to work closely with PhD student Xintong Wang and have the mentorship of Professor Michael P. Wellman.

---

Most recently, our research paper "Learning-Based Trading Strategies in the Face of Market Manipulation" has been accepted to [MAL - ICML 2019](https://sites.google.com/view/mal-icml-2019).
