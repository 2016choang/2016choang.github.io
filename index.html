Chris Hoang

Hi! I am a machine learning engineer at The Voleon Group, where I work on analyzing and modeling financial data.

Previously, I completed my bachelor's and master's in computer science at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I conducted research with the Michigan Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, where I was fortunate enough to work with Professor Honglak Lee on deep reinforcement learning and Professor Michael P. Wellman on multi-agent systems.

Research

I am interested in deep reinforcement learning, transfer learning, and meta learning. My research goal is to build agents that can tackle novel scenarios with greater autonomy and efficiency by generalizing prior experience.
